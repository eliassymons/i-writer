About Draftly

Welcome to Draftly — your creative companion for storytelling, editing, and character development. Whether you're crafting a novel, polishing a short story, or building a fictional world, Writing Assistant equips you with intuitive tools and AI-powered features to streamline your writing journey.

The Editor: Our intelligent editor empowers you to draft and revise with precision. Highlight any portion of text to receive instant AI suggestions that improve clarity, structure, and flow. With grammar refinement, tone adjustments, and alternative phrasing options, the editor helps elevate your prose in real time. You can also save and manage your drafts with ease using the built-in draft organizer.

The Character Designer: Bring your characters to life with our character design tool. Build rich profiles by filling out key traits such as name, age, appearance, personality, and backstory. For an added layer of creativity, you can generate AI-powered character portraits based on your descriptions—perfect for visualizing protagonists, villains, or entire casts.

Whether you're a novelist, screenwriter, RPG storyteller, or world-builder, Draftly gives you the structure to stay organized and the freedom to create. And when you're ready to share or archive your work, exporting options like PDF ensure your stories look great off-screen, too.

Start exploring Draftly today and unlock a smarter, more inspired way to write.
